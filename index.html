Mighty Knights' Chess Club

About

The Mighty Knights’ Chess Club is a place were all are welcome to come do battle on the chessboard. Whether you are a newbie or a pro, we would love to have you join us! We have regular meetups as well as host USCF rated tournaments. We started because we love the game of chess and found that the camaraderie, mental exercise, and competition to be a great benefit in our lives. We cultivate an environment of learning and good natured competition. Come find out how you join in on the fun!

Events

Types of events include:

  • Regular meetups
  • Unrated tournaments
  • USCF rated tournaments
  • Weekend workshops
  • Travel team
  • Tutoring
  • Classes
  • Online matches

Join Us

We are accepting new member! Send us your information and we will start the process. We offer regular meetups as well as the opportunity to participate in USCF rated tournaments. Someone will follow up with you within a couple of business days.
